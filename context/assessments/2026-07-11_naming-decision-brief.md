# Naming Decision Brief — the product name (one turn to rule)

## §0. The REC

**Ratify: the product ships as `Asimtote Core`, under the already-locked parent `Asimtote`.** Styling per the settled dual-register rule: `Asimtote` / `asimtote` for the company and wordmark, "Asimtote Core" as the product string, "an Asimtote product" as the footer/legal line (replacing W-7's "a NexSys product"). Consumer-warm edition names (the lane-B coinages) stay a deliberately-open option for a later mass-market edition — deferred, not rejected.

**Fallback (if attorney clearance fails on ASIMTOTE): `Havenkor`** — which this pass's fresh screens graduate over the 2026-07-05 doc's #1 (`Hearthkor`); see §1 rows 2–3 for the new evidence.

**The three decisive reasons:**

1. **It is the only candidate that carries the thesis for free.** The asymptote metaphor — approaches the ideal ever more closely and never claims to have reached it — is not adjacent to the positioning, it IS the positioning the acceptance arc just certified: never-false-CONFIRMED, honest UNCONFIRMED-with-reason, zero false CONFIRMs across ~25 verdicts (positioning notes §3, bench record). Every other candidate (warmth, refuge, home-center, neural-connectedness) names a *category feeling*; this one names *our differentiator*, and it arrives pre-loaded rather than needing years of meaning-filling.
2. **Every check that can be green is green, and this pass found a NEW flaw in each alternative.** Asimtote: domains already owned (asimtote.com live-verified registered 2026-07-10; strategy doc records Nick's ownership + variants), zero exact-match USPTO-indexed marks surfaced (screen, 2026-07-10), no GitHub-org/npm collisions surfaced, and the two known neighbors are managed, not ignored: the Cambridge PyPI tool (identical string; engineered around by distribution naming — the product ships as a deb/image, never `pip install`) and **Asimptote B.V.** (one letter away, software — §3's likelier clearance-failure vector, already the named attorney question). Meanwhile the fresh screens surfaced: a NASDAQ-listed software company homophone for Hearthkor (HeartCore Enterprises), a phonetic-twin field for Havenkor (HavenCORE), class-9 EMBER crowding + a Hungarian dictionary-word reading for Emberkor, and dense SYNAPSE-formative class-9 crowding plus two in-ecosystem "synapse" collisions for the incumbent (§1).
3. **It is the cheapest ratification the project will ever be offered, and the price rises monotonically from here.** The website build is already name-tokenized (`{{productName}}`/`{{companyName}}` substitution — W-11 rename-readiness, ruled into the Astro build 2026-07-03); nothing public has shipped (the site ships `noindex` until W-5); no second domain, trademark, or namespace tax is ever paid because editions extend by descriptor (Core/Home/Pro — the branded-house architecture the 2026-07-05 strategy doc recommends and this ruling would simultaneously ratify). The blast radius that remains is internal and phaseable (§2) — and every public surface that ships pre-ratification converts a token swap into a public rename. That is exactly why naming blocks every public surface.

**What this ruling is NOT:** it does not reopen the company name (`Asimtote` locked 2026-06-13; this pass's screens *confirm* that lock rather than contest it), does not pick the future consumer-edition name, and does not authorize spend past the §2 Phase-0 checklist until attorney clearance returns.

## §3. The REC's risks, stated honestly

1. **The radio test never passes; it's the concept's permanent tax.** Cold listeners will misspell it first try. Accepted knowingly (2026-06-13) for a prosumer/HA-refugee launch audience; the mitigations (house pronunciation, spell-on-air, wordmark pairing) are standing obligations, not one-time fixes. If the launch audience were mass-market-first, this REC would be weaker — it isn't, per the ratified segment model.
2. **Screen ≠ clearance, and the two named neighbors are real.** Asimptote B.V. (software, one letter away, `.com` live) and the Cambridge `asimtote` tool (identical string, same metaphor, active as of 2026-07-10) both sit in software. A clearance attorney could return a confusing-similarity opinion that forces the fallback — which is precisely why the fallback row is kept at the same screen depth as the REC, with row 2's own caveats standing (registrar-grade domain confirms and premium-tier status remain outstanding there too). I am not a lawyer; nothing above is clearance.
3. **Single-mark concentration.** The branded house hangs Core/Home/Pro/Enterprise off ONE fanciful mark — maximum leverage (D-2) and a single point of failure. Priced: two independent fallbacks exist at different layers — `asymtote` (drop-p) remains the recorded company-mark clearance fallback (2026-06-13 assessment), **fresh-screened this pass**: `asymtote.com` REGISTERED on the same GoDaddy NS family as `asimtote.com` ([dns.google](https://dns.google/resolve?name=asymtote.com&type=NS), 2026-07-10 — plausibly among Nick's recorded defensive "variants"; add it to the one-minute registrar confirm alongside homesynapse.com), and no exact ASYMTOTE mark surfaced (Justia/uspto.report search, 2026-07-10; nearest formative neighbor: ASYMPTOTE GENETIC MEDICINES, [SN 98915324](https://uspto.report/TM/98915324), far-field). `Havenkor` is this brief's product-name fallback. Phase-0 spend stays deliberately small until clearance returns.
4. **A warmth ceiling later.** "Asimtote Core" is infrastructural by design; a future mass-market edition may still want a warm consumer name (lane B stays open). Deferring that name is reversible; minting it now would pay the coined-name tax twice before there are two products (the 2026-07-05 doc's own logic, which this pass re-confirms with sharper collision evidence against the -kor set).
5. **The unverified residue (all minutes-level at ruling time):** GitHub org/social-handle claims (race risk), RDAP-grade domain confirms for the fallback set, the homesynapse.com and asymtote.com registrant presumptions, PyPI/npm API-level 404s. Each is flagged in §1 with what to do; none is load-bearing for §0.
