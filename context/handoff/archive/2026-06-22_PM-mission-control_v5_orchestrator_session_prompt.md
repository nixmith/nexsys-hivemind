## 0. The ambition (grasp this before the mechanics)

**The product is the demo, and the demo is the differentiator made visible.** A stranger installs HomeSynapse, pairs a motion sensor and a light, watches an automation fire, clicks **"why did this fire?"**, and **understands the answer** — and can also ask **"why DIDN'T it fire?"** and **"did the light actually confirm it turned on?"** The 2026-06-21 competitive research proved two gaps **no competitor fills**: the non-firing distinction (condition-false vs trigger-never-matched vs device-didn't-act) and command-outcome honesty (`dispatched → confirmed | unconfirmed | failed` — every other platform shows "command sent" and hides behind an optimistic lie). Lead on **INV-SA-03: the explanation is never evicted** — the #1 trace complaint in the field structurally cannot happen to us, because explainability is a pure projection of the immutable, causally-chained log (no parallel trace store).

**Why this is the best smart-home system on the market — and how it scales.** Local-first, inviolate; a trust brand that never leads with commodity encryption and never ingests person-linked data before the at-rest cipher is live (AB-4); reliability as a *provable product property* (your automations don't break on an update — the immutable log + no-destructive-migration). And the same single runtime is built to scale from a **free single-home user → paid power user → business/enterprise (multi-site / MDU / hospitality)** — **not a fork.** The Locked architecture already **reserves the tier seams**: federation/identity-scoping (Doc 16 §3.5, INV-SA-02 non-preclusion), the enterprise audit projection (Doc 16 §3.3, default-off), component-authoring (AX-7), crypto-shred. **The discipline that keeps those cheap to add later is that every V1 milestone stays NON-PRECLUDING of them** — so when a paid/enterprise milestone is finally scheduled it is a projection/extension over existing events, never a re-architecture. You build V1's thin slice; you protect the upscale runway with the invariants. Everything sequences backward from the demo, and forward into that vision.
