## What the leg must prove (the contract)

RX→trigger→dispatch through the REOPENED transport, on the frozen read surface (rider 1 unchanged: API-first; no sqlite, no log-scraping-as-API): **a run that did not exist at the reopen act's start, whose `triggeredAt` postdates the observed reopen, whose causal chain executed through the command pipeline.** A trigger IS an RX proof (the occupancy report arrived through the reopened port); an executed chain IS a TX proof (dispatches rode it).
