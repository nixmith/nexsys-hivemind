## The founder-referral channel (yours to run; can swap out any name above)

Ask any founder you respect who has a registered trademark: *"Who did your trademark, would you use them again, and did they ever argue you out of something?"* The third clause is the filter — you're looking for counsel who pushes back. If a referral surfaces, run the same conflicts question first, send the same email body, and vet on the same scorecard. A warm referral that passes vetting outranks a cold candidate that merely looks good on paper.
