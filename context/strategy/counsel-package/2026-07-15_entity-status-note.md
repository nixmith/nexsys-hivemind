## The question for counsel (memo Part 11 Q6, expanded)

The US application should be filed at the earliest date the clearance opinion allows — possibly before an entity exists. Which sequencing do you recommend?

1. **Form the LLC first, file in the LLC's name.** Cleanest chain of title; costs days-to-weeks of priority date depending on state processing (expedited options exist). Sub-questions: home-state formation vs DE/WY for a bootstrapped single-member company; any reason the trademark answer differs from the general corporate answer?
2. **File in the founder's personal name now; assign to the entity later.** Locks priority immediately. Sub-questions: §1(b) intent-to-use applications have statutory limits on assignment before the Statement of Use (anti-trafficking, 15 U.S.C. §1060(a)(1) — successor-to-the-business exception) — does that constrain this path enough to prefer option 1? Recordation mechanics and cost? Anything about this that complicates later investor diligence?
3. Any third structure you'd recommend for a founder who intends: single operating company, branded house (parent mark ASIMTOTE / consumer mark TAMORO), consumer hardware eventually, possible outside investment later.
